About Us

Air quality is a critical environmental factor affecting the well-being of urban populations worldwide. The Air Quality Index (AQI) serves as a crucial metric for assessing air quality, incorporating various pollutant concentrations into a single numerical value.

Machine Learning

Accurate AQI prediction is essential for proactive environmental management and public health protection. In recent years, machine learning and deep learning techniques have emerged as powerful tools for forecasting AQI values, leveraging data from diverse sources such as meteorological, geographical, and historical air quality data.
